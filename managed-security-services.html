The ongoing challenge

Security posture changes every month.

New users, devices, applications, sharing activity and configuration changes can create risk after the initial security project is complete.

01

Security drift

Controls may weaken over time as settings, licences and business processes change.

02

New risks

Threats, applications and user behaviour introduce new exposure continuously.

03

Alert overload

Teams may receive many alerts without a clear process to prioritize and act.

04

Access accumulation

Users and administrators may retain permissions that are no longer needed.

05

Limited internal capacity

Small teams may not have enough time to review security settings regularly.

06

Unclear reporting

Leadership may not receive simple information about risk, progress and priorities.
